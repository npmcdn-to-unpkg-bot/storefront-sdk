Storefront SDK

Libraries

This document lists all the libraries that SDK provides and its versions.

  • alt at v0.17.4, available at window.alt
  • axios at v0.7.0, available at window.axios
  • history at v1.12.0, instance available at window.storefront.sdk.history
  • Immutable at v3.7.5, available at window.Immutable
  • Intl at v1.0.0, available at window.Intl
  • React at v0.14.0, available at window.React
  • ReactDOM at v0.14.0, available at window.ReactDOM
  • React Intl at v1.2.0, available at window.ReactIntl
  • React Router at v1.0.0-rc2, available at window.ReactRouter

To make the best use of them, add to your webpack.config.js:

externals: {
  'alt': 'alt',
  'axios': 'axios',
  'immutable': 'Immutable',
  'intl': 'Intl',
  'react': 'React',
  'react-intl': 'ReactIntl',
  'react-router': 'ReactRouter',
  'sdk': 'storefront.sdk'
},
